ABOUT OUR STORY

AV Laundry has been around since March 2017. It was originally a regular laundry we rented a house for. We barely had any employees and a few washing machines

After a few months, around October we had a pretty significant increase in business as well as improvements in many different areas. We believe it was due to customer's satisfaction of our given services. We also had more employees working for us and more washing machines to serve even more customers

at the time it makes us so confident to start this business. Started many customers that come, not only that, we also start our old place in the famous. One of our customers suggest why don't you make a bigger place

We then decided to find a better place for our business. However, our new place is quite distant to the old one. Thus our regular customers had a hard time using our services due to natural increase of transportation fee. Sadly this cost us a drop in customer and orders rate

And that's when we learned our lesson. We decided to create a website to expand our services and make it easier for our customers to use our services. Through this website, we can promote our services and customers can easily order our services in a timely manner
